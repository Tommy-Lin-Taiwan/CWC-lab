Congratulations to Prof. Chun-Wei Chen for being elected to the Fellow of the School of Engineering, University of Tokyo

2023.12

Prof. Chun-Wei Chen was elected as a Fellow of the School of Engineering, University of Tokyo, 2022. Due to COVID, the award ceremony was held on 12/7, 2023, in NTU and was given by Prof. Satoshi Watanabe and Kosuke Nagashio from the University of Tokyo.
