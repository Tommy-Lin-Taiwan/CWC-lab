Our Group

Welcome to Professor Chun-Wei Chen's lab. The research activities in this lab are focusing on two major areas.

(1) Novel two-dimensional atomic layer materials

(2) Next-generation energy materials based on atomic or nano-materials and devices

We welcome motivated postdocs, graduate students (master/Ph.D.), and undergraduates with a solid background and deep interests in the fundamental science behind new materials as well as their application to join us.
