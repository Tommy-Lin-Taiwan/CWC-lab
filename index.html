Our Group

Welcome to professor Chun-Wei Chen's lab. The research activities in this lab are focusing on two major areas.

(1) Graphene and novel two-dimensional atomic layer materials.

(2) Next-generation energy materials based on novel nano-materials and devices

We welcome interested master / PhD students and undergraduates with solid engineering background and deep interest in the fundamental physics behind new materials as well as their application to join us.
